GALLERIA-DE-ARTISTE

With Corona Virus taking over the world like aliens in a sci-fi movie, all of us have been confined to our homes. The trouble began when mental ill-health began to strike. We started getting hit by: Stress Anxiety Depression Boredom Unwillingness to work With Doctors, Policemen, being our Avengers, and fighting against the virus, we the people, sitting at home on our couches have nothing better to do. In the meantime, during these 3 difficult months, we have also witnessed many people trying their best to sharpen or build up skills. It could be anything, from drawing a simple sketch to designing a complicated website(wink wink). That is where Galleria-De-Artiste comes in. Read below to know more!
